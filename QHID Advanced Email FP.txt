Subject: Understanding Hypertension and Managing Blood Pressure

Dear Member,

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a condition that affects millions of individuals worldwide. Understanding how to manage blood pressure effectively can significantly improve overall health.

### What is Hypertension?
- Hypertension is defined as a consistent blood pressure reading above 130/80 mmHg.
- It can lead to serious health complications such as heart disease, stroke, and kidney damage if left untreated.

### Managing Your Blood Pressure:
- Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
- Engage in regular physical activity, such as walking or swimming.
- Monitor your blood pressure regularly using a home monitor.
- Reduce sodium intake by avoiding processed foods.
- Manage stress through relaxation techniques, such as meditation.
